Why Walk?

The Problem:


It's the last leg of your commute, you're two blocks from your destination, maybe you're even in the building already. Now what? Until today you'd have to walk those last couple blocks, climb that flight of stairs, or push that button on the elevator like a barbarian.

The Solution:


Thanks to Human Bixie, your troubles are over. Our dedicated service professionals (mulemen ©) will carry you the rest of the way! You don't have to lift a finger.

FAQ'S

there is no limit on what kind of terrain you can ask your muleman to traverse, but for your own comfort, you might want to put a towel between you and them on those extra long hikes.
